Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

StressLessMom (hereinafter "we") takes your privacy protection very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information Collection

Information We Collect

  • Training Data: Your breathing training records, training duration, completion counts, and other data are stored only locally on your device.
  • Settings Preferences: Your app settings (such as default training duration, haptic feedback toggle, etc.) are stored only locally on your device.
  • Notification Permissions: Training reminder notifications are sent only with your consent.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Network usage data

Information Usage

Local Storage

All data is stored only locally on your device. We do not upload any data to any servers.

Notification Function

  • Local notifications are sent only when you enable reminder features
  • Notification content includes only training reminders, no personal information

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties because:

  • All data is stored only locally on your device
  • We do not collect personal information that needs to be shared

Data Security

  • All data uses iOS system-provided secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is accessible only on your device
  • We do not use network transmission for your data

Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically designed for children under 13, nor do we intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app with the update date noted.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

  • Email: support@stresslessmom.app
  • In-app feedback function

Consent

Using our app indicates your agreement to the terms of this privacy policy.
